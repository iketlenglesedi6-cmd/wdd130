Beginner Float

Perfect for first-timers and families, this 2-hour trip gently floats down calm rivers. Enjoy scenic views, expert guidance, and small rapids that provide a taste of adventure without stress. A great way to introduce kids or anyone new to rafting.

Intermediate Rapids

Step up your adventure with 3 hours of moderate rapids and exciting river twists. Experience fun drops and small waves while spotting riverside wildlife. Ideal for groups, friends, and anyone looking for a bit more thrill while staying safe.

Thunder Gorge Challenge

Not for the faint-hearted: 4 hours of class IV–V rapids in a dramatic gorge setting. Feel the adrenaline rush as you navigate powerful waves, steep drops, and roaring rapids. Expert guides ensure safety while delivering the ultimate whitewater experience for thrill-seekers.
